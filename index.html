About Us

North Macedonia’s First Official Car Recycling Company

We are proud to lead the automotive recycling industry in North Macedonia, operating with full authorization and strict environmental compliance. Our mission is to recover, repurpose, and recycle vehicles in a way that supports sustainability and a circular economy.

Comprehensive Recycling Solutions

From dismantling to material recovery, we take care of every step. Our process ensures that over 95% of a vehicle is reused or recycled — meeting European Union eco-standards. Safety, efficiency, and sustainability are at the core of our operation.

Commitment to the Environment

We don’t just recycle cars — we reduce waste, conserve resources, and prevent pollution. Our certified processes ensure eco-friendly dismantling and disposal, helping to build a cleaner, greener North Macedonia.

Driving the Future

Innovation drives us forward. We aim to continuously evolve our technology and processes to serve as a model for sustainable vehicle recycling across the Balkans.

Our Services

Eco Vehicle Dismantling

Environmentally responsible dismantling of end-of-life vehicles, ensuring minimal waste.

Used Car Parts Supply

Quality tested second-hand car parts for various makes and models.

Scrap Metal Recycling

We recycle all types of automotive metals and reduce landfill usage.

Oil & Fluid Recycling

Safe extraction and processing of engine oils and car fluids to prevent pollution.
